About Kuberan's House

The Kuberan’s Foundation is the pivot of the ideation and the nucleus organization which invites, develops, streamlines and connects Business ideas and inventions with prospective investors and Venture capitalists.

The Kuberan’s Foundation has 3 verticals:

  • The Fellowship
  • The Foundry
  • The Factory

each with its own function and objectives. The 3 arms align together in a seamless process to execute sourcing ideators to finally connecting them to the investors.

The Foundation & Fellowship The Foundry The Forum

The Foundation is the Central Core of the concept and is the front end of the Kuberan House constituted by the core team and aided by the best professionals in P.R, Digital Marketing, Advertising and advisors to the best technical institutes across India.

The aim is to reach out throughout India and invite applications from ideators and start up entrepreneurs with innovative ideas looking for funding to establish a business model. The team would go all out using all forms of PR machinery like Social Media, Print , Press , Television, Radio etc to reach every nook and corner of India especially B centres in rural India and create awareness of the platform helping to fulfil the dreams of every innovator/ Ideator who does not have a platform. A comprehensive website will have details of the concept, the process, profiles of venture capitalists on board along with a detailed downloadable application form. Each such application form has be filled in detail with the technical and financial specs of the start up idea and has to be mailed to the foundation.

Extensive publicity using every media tool possible along with an access to database from technical institutions, who will be sent direct mailers will ensure maximum and the best ideator participation from across the length and breadth of India. The Kuberans House will also have a well known popular celebrity Brand Ambassador who will feature in all the multimedia activities planned to popularise the concept and help maximise the response qualitatively and quantitavely across India. The Brand Ambassador would also double up as the Grand master on the televised show and would initiate and arbitrate the deals between the Ideators and the investors. He would be aided by a Glamorous popular Female hostess.

All online application go through various stages of filtering process which includes exhaustive checks on Background, Tech specs, Patent and originality and the potential feasibility of the proposed business idea. The shortlist is done by an expert in house panel of technical and business professionals.

The top 500 applications which pass through the stringent parameters are empaneled in the ‘Hall of Fame’ start ups every season and each of them are handed out The ‘Kuberan’s Fellowship’ certificate as a seal of Merit. After further scrutiny, the very best from the hall of Fame the ‘Kuberan’s Fellowship’ are handpicked based on credentials of Originality and Feasibility are called in to be part of the Kuberans Foundry.

The Foundry is a holistic and an exhaustive workshop conducted for each of the Top ‘Kuberans Fellowship’ member who are invited to be a part of a 3 day comprehensive initiation programme.

Consisting of an elitist panel from the core team and Professionals in Corporate finance, Strategy, Chartered accounts, Fund management, Multi media and Public speaking, the workshop for the ‘Kuberan Fellowship’ members aims to project, explore and impart each and every aspect of making a successful pitch to the investors.

The Foundry is a complete grooming program specially designed for the ideators to pitch their idea as an effective business module and assist in optimising its marketability. All the Ideators go through group discussions, seminars, one on one meets and are specifically groomed on creating effective presentation, Look & appeal and the adding to the entertainment quotient of the final show.

The top Ideators from the programme are awarded in a closing ceremony with distinguished celebrities.

The Core team finally handpicks the top 100 Ideators based on merit of the idea and the performance of the ideators in the ‘Foundry Workshop’.

The 100 top ideators are informed of the final Shortlist for being featured on the show and the final process and logistics of presenting them is handled by the Core team.

The Forum is the Final Showcase and the front end of the Kuberan’s House. Set in a Palatial luxury Lounge in the midst of a Warehouse, The Kuberan’s House acts as a well decked and luxurious magnificent backdrop for the investors, termed as Kuberas on the show.

A Luxury lounge, a discussion suite and the Pitching Hall are all part of the elegantly designed Kuberan’s House.

The Pitch Hall has Specially designed Chairs for Each of the Kuberas facing a large stage with Display kiosks and LED walls for the Ideators to pitch, exhibit and explain their ideas.

The Kuberan’s House is mentioned as a Strictly Private place of the Kuberas, with Entry by Invitation Only. Sonu Sood would roleplay the moderator between the Ideators and the Kuberas in each episode, giving access to only the Top 60 ideators shorlisted in the Foundry.

Aided by a glamorous show hostess, he invites and introduces each ideator to the Kuberas. Seated in a special seating between the Kuberas and the ideator, he would initiate and moderate every Pitch in the Kuberan’s House.

The Kuberas are the absolute Masters of the Show and control the proceedings and the pitches in their very own style and manner. Their decision is final and binding to all ideators and pitches on the show.
