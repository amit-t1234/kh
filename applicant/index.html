    About kuberan’s house

    kuberan’s house acts as a bridge between enterpreniur and investors.

    KH creates an opportunity for enterpreneurs to present their ideas to a panel of investors, the “Kuberas” of the show and pitch for financial investment while offering a share of the business in return

    Are you an deator/Entrepreneur ?

    Kuberans House is an ideation of a perfect Platform to Source, Groom and Streamline innovative Startups ideas and connect them to venture capitalists/ Investors using the medium of television, thus turning them into Successful Business ventures The show aims to Showcase the most innovative original Indian business ideas and help the ideators incubate, negotiate and finalise deals with wholly Indian investors embodying the spirit of the ‘Make in India’ movement.

    The Kuberan’s Foundation is the pivot of the ideation and the nucleus organization which invites, develops, streamlines and connects Business ideas and inventions with prospective investors and Venture capitalists.
